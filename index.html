OUR TARGET
Connect - Invest - Shape Future
  • Synergy & Elevation
    Strengthen connections and establish a shared direction among golf course owners in Vietnam to elevate service standards and enhance the competitive position of Vietnam's golf tourism industry in the international market.
  • Alliance Formation
    Lay the groundwork for establishing the Vietnam Golf Course Owners Alliance, fostering cooperation and building long-term strategic relationships within the industry.
  • Sustainable Development
    Champion sustainable development activities as an inseparable core element of the national golf development strategy, ensuring alignment with global trends.
  • Connection & Promotion
    Create a bridge between stakeholders, attract foreign investment, and promote Vietnam's image as a leading golf destination in the region and worldwide.
